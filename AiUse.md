# ✅ **5 Prompts: Use AI the RIGHT Way (Build Logic, Not Dependency)**

---

### **1️⃣ “Explain this code line by line without rewriting it. I want to understand the logic, not copy.”**

**Hindi:**
*Yeh tumhari understanding ko deep banata hai.*

---

### **2️⃣ “Break this problem into smaller logical steps. I will solve each step myself.”**

**Why:** Teaches decomposition → expert-level thinking.

---

### **3️⃣ “Here is my code. Don’t fix it. Just tell me where my thinking went wrong.”**

**Hindi:**
*Galti samajhna = fastest growth.*

---

### **4️⃣ “Give me 3 alternative ways an expert developer might think about this problem.”**

**Why:** Builds pattern recognition + creative logic.

---

### **5️⃣ “Explain the underlying concept behind this error. Don’t give the solution yet.”**

**Hindi:**
*Error ka mool samajhna developer ki asli skill hoti hai.*

---

# ❌ **5 Prompts: DO NOT Use AI This Way (Destroys Logic)**

---

### **6️⃣ “Write the entire solution/code for me.”**

Reason: Full dependency, zero thinking.

---

### **7️⃣ “Give me the answer directly without explanation.”**

**Hindi:**
*Seedha answer mil gaya toh dimaag kaam karna band karta hai.*

---

### **8️⃣ “Fix my code completely.”**

Why: You skip the debugging process → no growth.

---

### **9️⃣ “Do the logic for me, I don’t know how to start.”**

Correct approach = ask for hints, not logic replacement.

---

### **🔟 “Give me a shortcut to solve this problem without understanding the concept.”**

**Hindi:**
*Shortcuts aaj bachayenge, kal barbaad karenge.*

---
